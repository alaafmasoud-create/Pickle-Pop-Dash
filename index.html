Ready to dash?

Move the pickle, dodge the falling chaos, collect stars, and beat your best score.

How to play

  • Move with ← and → or the on-screen controls.
  • Hold Shift or press Boost for a short speed burst.
  • Dodge the falling hazards.
  • Collect stars for bonus points.
  • Grab shields to survive one hit.

Game features

  • Works online in any modern browser
  • Keyboard + touch controls
  • Local high score memory
  • Progressive difficulty scaling
  • No external assets required

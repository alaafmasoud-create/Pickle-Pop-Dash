How to Play

Controls

  • Move with ← and → or the on-screen buttons.
  • Hold Shift or press Boost for a short speed burst.
  • Press Space to start or pause.
  • Press F for fullscreen, M for sound effects, and use Skin to switch pickle styles.

Goal

  • Dodge the falling hazards as long as you can.
  • Collect stars for bonus points.
  • Grab shields to survive one hit.
  • Try different pickle skins for a fresh look.
  • The pace gets faster as your level rises.

Tip

For the best experience, use fullscreen and keep music on for the playful arcade vibe.
